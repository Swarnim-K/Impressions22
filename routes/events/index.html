Impressions is the annual international Cultural Festival of the College of Engineering Pune Technological University - COEP Tech. We are a fest that strives to live by our tagline "By the artist, for the artist", currently in our 7th edition. We at Impressions believe in providing a voice for a plethora of talents and an all-encompassing platform for artists to show off their skills. Stay tuned for our much-awaited Seventh Edition!

COEP Tech is one of the oldest Engineering institutions in Asia, with a rich history that dates back over 150 years. With a Cultural club within the institute having a century-old heritage, Impressions takes great pride in carrying this glorious tradition forward.

Come DM us on our social media handles to be a part of our various campaigns, workshops and competitions that shall take place in the run up to and during our main fest from the 22nd to 24th of December 2022 !
